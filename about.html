🐾 Our Story

It Started in a Small Studio in Yiwu

Yiwu, China — the global capital of small commodities. Millions of products flow through this city every day, most anonymous and interchangeable. We asked: what if a plush toy had a name, a personality, a soul?

Handcrafted, Not Mass-Produced

Every Mochidog is assembled and finished by hand. Our artisans adjust each of the 9 joints individually — so your companion sits, stands, and poses exactly right. They embroider the facial features one at a time. They shape the curls, set the ears, inspect every stitch.

This is not a factory line. This is a small atelier where each companion matters. A single Mochidog takes hours of focused handwork from start to finish.

An Identity, Not a SKU

We give every Mochidog a name, a personality, a birthday, and a story. Not because a machine generated it — but because our artisans believed each one deserved it. The name Sunny Biscuit isn't a random string. It's who she is.

The Adoption Philosophy

We use the word "adoption" because we believe these are companions, not commodities. Our customers name them, dress them, take them on trips, and write us letters about how their Mochidog helped them through hard times.

🐾 Welcome to Mochidog. Seven companions are waiting. One of them is yours.
