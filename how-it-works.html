🐾 How Adoption Works

🐕

1. Choose Your Plush Puppy

Browse our collection of 21 unique poseable plush toy puppies. Each has their own style, color, and personality preview. Find the stuffed companion that speaks to your heart.

📦

2. Receive the Adoption Box

Your puppy arrives in a beautifully crafted box with their first outfit set, adoption code card, branded collar, metal ID tag, and a surprise accessory.

📱

3. Scan to Unlock

Scan the unique adoption code inside your box. Our AI instantly generates your puppy's one-of-a-kind identity: name, birth story, personality, special quirks, and a custom portrait.

💕

4. Share & Love

Share your adoption story on social media. Dress up your puppy with our outfit collections. Join our community of puppy parents. Your puppy's story is just beginning.

The AI Magic ✨

Every Mochidog puppy comes with a unique scannable code. When scanned, our AI generates:

  • 🏷️ A one-of-a-kind name (like "Sunny Biscuit" or "Lucky Mochi")
  • 📖 A 500-word birth story written in warm, fairytale style
  • 🌟 A personality profile with character traits
  • 🐾 Special quirks and talents unique to them
  • 📜 A printable adoption certificate with their official Mochidog number

No two puppies are alike. Ever.
