🐾 Our Story

In a small studio in Yiwu, China, 21 special puppies were born — but not ordinary ones.

Each Mozhi puppy has movable joints, changeable outfits, and most importantly — a one-of-a-kind AI-generated identity. A name. A personality. A birth story.

We believe toys shouldn't just sit on shelves. They should have souls.

When you adopt a Mozhi puppy and scan the code, you unlock their entire world: who they are, where they came from, and the tiny quirks that make them uniquely yours.

This isn't shopping. This is meeting your new best friend.
