🐾 Our Story

In a small studio in Yiwu, China — the plush toy capital of the world — 7 one-of-a-kind plush dogs came to life.

Each Mochidog is a poseable plush doll with 9 articulated joints, changeable outfits, and — most importantly — an AI-generated identity. A unique name. A personality. A full backstory, created just for that one plush companion.

We believe stuffed toys should be more than just shelf decorations. They should have character.

When you bring home a Mochidog and scan its code, you unlock its entire world: who it is, where it came from, and the tiny quirks that make it uniquely yours.

Start your collection today — every Mochidog is a new story waiting to be discovered.
