↩️ Refund Policy

Returns

We accept returns within 14 days of delivery. Items must be in original, unused condition with all packaging and accessories.

Damaged Items

If your puppy arrives damaged, contact us within 7 days with photos. We'll send a replacement immediately at no cost.

How to Return

Email hello@mochidog.store with your order number and reason for return. We'll provide return instructions.

Refunds

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive the returned item. Shipping costs are non-refundable.

Exchanges

Want a different puppy? Contact us and we'll help you find the right match. Exchange shipping is on us.
