❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Are these real dogs?

No — Mochidogs are handcrafted poseable plush art dolls, not live animals. They're artisan companions made from fabric, with 9 articulated joints and hand-finished details. Think of them as collectible textile art pieces with personality.

What does "adoption" mean?

Adoption is our philosophy, not a gimmick. When you adopt a Mochidog, you receive a hand-packed collector box with their first outfit, a scannable identity code, and a numbered certificate. Scan the code and our AI reveals their unique name, origin story, personality, and secret quirks — an identity that belongs only to them. We call it adoption because we believe these are companions, not commodities.

Are they really handcrafted?

Yes. Every Mochidog is assembled and finished by hand in our Yiwu studio. Joints are individually tensioned, facial features are hand-embroidered, and details like Sugar Puff's blush gradient or Coco Cloud's textured curls are achieved through manual processes unique to each piece. No two are exactly alike.

Is the AI identity really unique for each one?

Absolutely. Our AI considers each Mochidog's breed style, color, and visual details to generate completely original identities. Even two Mochidogs of the same style will receive different names, stories, and personality profiles. The same model can produce millions of distinct identities.

Can I change their outfits?

Yes! All Mochidogs are designed with changeable outfits. Our wardrobe collection includes 5 outfit sets, plus hats, sunglasses, and shoes — all hand-sewn in small batches.

How big are they?

Approximately 30cm (12 inches) tall. Outfits and accessories are designed specifically for this size.

How long does shipping take?

US: 7-15 business days. Thailand: 5-10 business days. UK: 10-18 business days. All orders include tracking. Free shipping on orders over $59.

What if my Mochidog arrives damaged?

We'll replace them immediately at no cost. Contact us within 7 days with photos — we take full responsibility for their safe arrival.

Can I return my Mochidog?

We accept returns within 14 days in original condition. But we should warn you — most people can't bring themselves to send them back once they've met.

Can I adopt more than one?

Many of our collectors adopt multiple Mochidogs! Each has a different personality and story, and they make wonderful companions for each other. Repeat adopters receive 10% off.

How are the joints made?

Each Mochidog has 9 articulated joints (head, shoulders, elbows, hips, knees) using durable ABS plastic connectors. Our artisans individually adjust the tension on every joint during assembly so your companion poses perfectly.
