🐾 How It Works

🐕

1. Pick Your Plush Dog

Browse our collection of 7 unique poseable plush dog dolls. Each has their own style, color, and personality preview. Find the one that catches your eye.

📦

2. Unbox Your Companion

Your plush dog arrives in a beautifully designed box with its first outfit set, an identity code card, branded collar, metal ID tag, and a surprise accessory.

📱

3. Unlock Its Identity

Scan the unique code inside your box. Our AI instantly generates your plush dog's one-of-a-kind identity: name, backstory, personality, special quirks, and a custom portrait.

💕

4. Collect & Share

Share your unboxing on social media. Dress up your plush dog with our outfit collections. Collect them all — each Mochidog is a new story.

The AI Magic ✨

Every Mochidog comes with a unique scannable code. When scanned, our AI generates:

  • 🏷️ A one-of-a-kind name (like "Sunny Biscuit" or "Lucky Mochi")
  • 📖 A 500-word backstory written in warm, fairytale style
  • 🌟 A personality profile with character traits
  • 🐾 Special quirks and talents unique to them
  • 📜 A printable certificate with their official Mochidog number

No two Mochidogs are alike. Ever.
