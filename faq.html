❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Are these real dogs?

No. Mochidogs are poseable plush/stuffed dolls — not live animals. They're collectible fabric dolls with 9 articulated joints and changeable outfits, each paired with a unique AI-generated identity. Think premium stuffed collectibles with a digital twist.

What's in the box?

Your Mochidog plush dog + first outfit set + branded collar & metal ID tag + identity code card + thank you card + surprise accessory.

What is the AI identity?

Every Mochidog comes with a unique scannable code. When scanned, our AI generates a one-of-a-kind name, backstory, personality profile, and digital portrait. No two Mochidogs share the same identity — even if they look identical.

Can I change the outfits?

Yes! All Mochidog plush dogs are designed with changeable outfits. Browse our Outfits & Accessories collection to build their wardrobe.

How big are they?

All Mochidog plush dogs are approximately 30cm (12 inches) tall. Outfits and accessories are designed to fit this size.

How long does shipping take?

US: 7-15 business days. Thailand: 5-10 business days. UK: 10-18 business days. All orders include tracking. Free shipping on orders over $59.

What if my plush dog arrives damaged?

We'll replace it free of charge. Just contact us within 7 days of delivery with photos.

Can I return it?

We accept returns within 14 days in original, unused condition. But fair warning — most people can't bear to part with them.

Can I collect more than one?

Absolutely! Many of our customers collect multiple Mochidogs. They love building a full collection. Repeat customers get 10% off.

How are the joints made?

Each plush dog has 9 articulated joints (head, shoulders, elbows, hips, knees) made with durable ABS plastic connectors. They hold poses well and are designed to withstand daily handling.

Is the AI identity really different for every single one?

Yes. Our AI draws from a massive creative database and considers each plush dog's breed style, color, and subtle visual details to generate truly unique identities. The same model can produce millions of different stories.
