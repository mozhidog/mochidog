❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Are these real dogs?

No — Mochidogs are handcrafted poseable plush art dolls, not live animals. They're artisan companions with 9 articulated joints and hand-finished details.

What does "adoption" mean?

It means we treat each Mochidog as an individual, not a commodity. When you adopt one, you receive a hand-packed box with their first outfit, branded collar, numbered certificate, and a surprise accessory.

Are they really handcrafted?

Yes. Every Mochidog is assembled and finished by hand in our Yiwu studio. Joints are individually tensioned, faces hand-embroidered, and details like curls and blush are achieved through manual processes that take hours per piece.

How long does each one take to make?

Depending on the design, 1-6 hours of focused handwork. Sugar Puff's blush gradient alone takes 40 minutes. Marshmallow Dream's dye process takes 6 hours.

Can I change their outfits?

Yes! All Mochidogs are designed with changeable outfits. Browse our Clothes collection — 10 hand-sewn outfits available.

How big are they?

Approximately 30cm (12 inches) tall. All outfits are designed for this size.

Shipping?

US: 7-15 days · Thailand: 5-10 days · UK: 10-18 days. Free shipping over $59.

Returns?

14 days in original condition. Damaged items replaced free within 7 days.

Can I collect more than one?

Many collectors adopt multiple Mochidogs! Each has a different personality and handcrafted details. Repeat customers get 10% off.
