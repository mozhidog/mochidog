❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Are these real dogs?

No. Mozhi puppies are poseable plush/stuffed toys — not live animals. They're collectible fabric dolls with movable joints and changeable outfits, each paired with a unique AI-generated story for fun. Think Build-a-Bear meets Jellycat, with a digital identity twist.

What's included in the adoption box?

Your Mozhi puppy + first outfit set + branded collar & metal ID tag + adoption code card + thank you card + surprise accessory. Everything you need to welcome them home.

What is the AI story?

Every puppy comes with a unique scannable code. When you scan it, our AI generates a one-of-a-kind name, birth story, personality profile, and digital portrait. No two puppies share the same identity.

Can I change the outfits?

Yes! All Mozhi puppies are designed with changeable outfits. Browse our Clothes & Accessories collection to build their wardrobe.

How long does shipping take?

US: 7-15 business days. Thailand: 5-10 business days. UK: 10-18 business days. All orders include tracking.

What if my puppy arrives damaged?

We'll replace it free of charge. Just contact us within 7 days of delivery with photos.

Can I return my puppy?

We accept returns within 14 days in original condition. But fair warning — most people can't bear to send them back.

Can I adopt more than one?

Absolutely! Many of our customers adopt 2-3 puppies. They love having a little family. Plus, repeat adopters get 10% off.

How are the joints made?

Each puppy has 9 articulated joints (head, shoulders, elbows, hips, knees) made with durable ABS plastic connectors. They hold poses well and are designed to withstand daily cuddles.

Is the AI story really different for every single puppy?

Yes. Our AI draws from a massive creative database and considers each puppy's breed style, color, and even subtle visual details to generate truly unique stories. The same puppy model can produce millions of different identities.
