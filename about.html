🐾 Our Story

It Started in a Small Studio in Yiwu

Yiwu, China — the global capital of small commodities. Millions of products flow through this city every day, most of them anonymous, interchangeable, forgotten the moment they're boxed.

We asked a different question: what if a plush toy wasn't anonymous? What if it had a name, a story, a soul?

Handcrafted, Not Mass-Produced

Every Mochidog is assembled and finished by hand in our studio. Our artisans individually adjust each of the 9 joints so they pose perfectly. They embroider the facial features one at a time. They shape the curls, set the ears, and inspect every stitch.

This is not a factory line. This is a small atelier where each companion matters.

An Identity, Not a SKU

We partnered with AI not to replace human creativity, but to amplify it. When you scan your Mochidog's code, our AI generates a completely unique identity — a name, an origin story, a personality profile, secret quirks — that belongs only to that one companion.

No two Mochidogs are alike. Not in their stitching, not in their curl patterns, not in their stories. Like snowflakes. Like people. Like the best things in life.

The Adoption Philosophy

We don't use the word "adoption" as a marketing gimmick. We use it because we believe the relationship between a person and their Mochidog is real. Our customers name them. They dress them. They take them on trips. They write us letters about how their Mochidog helped them through a hard time.

This is why we do what we do. Not to sell stuffed animals. To create companions.
