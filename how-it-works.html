🐾 The Adoption Experience

🐕

1. Find Your Companion

Browse our 7 artisan-crafted plush dogs. Each has a distinct look, personality preview, and handcraft story. When you feel that click — you'll know.

📦

2. The Arrival

Your companion arrives in a hand-packed collector box. Inside: the Mochidog you chose, wearing their first outfit and branded collar, plus an identity code card and a surprise accessory carefully selected for them.

📱

3. The Reveal

Scan the unique code. Our AI generates — in real time — their name, their origin story, their personality profile, their secret quirks and talents, and a numbered certificate. This identity belongs to them and them alone.

💕

4. A Life Together

Name them. Dress them. Take them everywhere. Share their story with our community. Collect their friends. Each Mochidog is a new character in your story.

The AI Identity ✨

When you scan your Mochidog's code, here's what our AI creates — uniquely, every time:

  • 🏷️ A one-of-a-kind name — like "Sunny Biscuit" or "Lucky Mochi" — that matches their look and personality
  • 📖 A 500-word origin story — written in a warm, fairytale style, telling the tale of how they came to be
  • 🌟 A personality profile — their character traits, temperament, and what kind of person they're waiting for
  • 🐾 Secret quirks & talents — the little things that make them who they are
  • 📜 A numbered adoption certificate — with their official Mochidog number, ready to frame

Even two Mochidogs that look identical will have completely different identities. The AI considers their breed style, color, and subtle visual details to create something truly original.
