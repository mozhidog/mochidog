🐾 The Adoption Experience

🐕

1. Find Your Companion

Browse 7 artisan-crafted plush dogs. Each has a distinct personality — when you feel that connection, you'll know.

📦

2. The Arrival

Your companion arrives in a hand-packed collector box. Inside: the Mochidog you chose, wearing their first outfit and branded collar, plus a numbered certificate and a surprise accessory selected just for them.

🤲

3. The First Meeting

Open the box. Meet your companion. Every Mochidog is hand-finished — the joint tension, the embroidered expression, the textured curls — all done by human hands. No two are exactly alike.

💕

4. A Life Together

Name them. Dress them. Take them everywhere. Share their story. Collect them all — each Mochidog is a new character in your story.

The Handcrafted Difference ✨

Every Mochidog takes hours of focused handwork:

  • 🤲 9 individually tensioned joints — so they pose perfectly, not too loose, not too stiff
  • 🧵 Hand-embroidered features — each face is stitched one at a time, not stamped by machine
  • ✂️ Hand-shaped details — textured curls, blush gradients, shaped ears — all done by artisans
  • 📜 Numbered certificate — because every Mochidog is a numbered piece, not a mass-produced unit

When you hold a Mochidog, you're holding hours of someone's careful work. That's the difference.
